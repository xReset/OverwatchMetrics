Frequently Asked Questions

What patch is this data from?

All stats are pulled from the start of the most recent patch. When a new update drops, it can take up to a day for the site to catch up.

How is Pick Rate calculated?

Pick Rate shows how often a Hero is played based on total playtime, compared to all Heroes combined. The more time spent on a Hero, the higher their pick rate climbs.

What do the -- values mean?

Those dashes pop up when there isnt enough data yet. That can happen with rarely picked Heroes, mirrored matchups, or when a patch is still fresh.

How is the Progress Bar Fill calculated?

The progress bar shows where a Hero stands between the lowest and highest data points think of it as a quick visual snapshot of performance.

Do all filters update the data in the table?

Almost! Every filter updates the data in real time except Role, which just hides other Heroes from view.
